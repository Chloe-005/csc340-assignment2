About This Website

This website was created to show a few characters from different anime. This site showcases different fictional characters and has information in a organized and easy to read way.

I chose an anime theme because I enjoy anime and I knew there was a lot of characters I could choose from. For this assignment, I wanted it to be easy to use and look nice. I choose blue since its a lot of people like that color. This layout seemed to me to be the most organized looking one so I chose it.
