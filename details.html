Asuna Yuuki

Character details

Universe: Sword Art Online

Role: Fighter

Power Level: High

Species: Human

Backstory

Asuna Yuuki is a skilled swordswoman trapped in the virtual reality game Sword Art Online. She becomes one of the strongest players and joins the Knights of the Blood Oath, where she helps lead the fight to free everyone from the game.

Abilities

  • Advanced sword fighting skills
  • Healing skills
  • Rapier Mastery

First Appearance

She first shows up in epiode one of the anime. She is a recurring character in Sword Art online.

Personality

She is kind, strong willed, and determined. These traits help her standout in Sword Art Online.
